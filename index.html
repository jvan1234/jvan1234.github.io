Why do you Represent yourself as a Changeling online?
Changelings, primarily in old folklore, were old fairies that replaced babies. These babies were often taken for three purposes: for malice, for servitude or for care.
The changeling then acts as the baby for the human family. The changeling had noticable flaws, which includes deformities, illness, or immobility (Source: Irish Post)

Changelings are often stand-ins for mental disorders and disabilities, such as ASD, ADHD, BPD, and much more. While I only have been confirmed for having ASD, I feel that the changeling is representative of how socially seperated ASD can make the individual feel and the masks they must put on to fit in.
